Origin
"A physics degree. An NBA draft pick. And the first thing he builds when he gets there — is a way back."

Before the businesses, there was the foundation. Andrew Nicholson was still playing in the NBA when he started 44 Hoops in 2014 — his jersey number, his hometown, his name on the door.

A Mississauga kid who made the league didn't disappear into it. He turned around. 44 Hoops is the venture that makes everything else make sense — proof that the platform was always meant to be pointed back at the community.

The 4 Fours

His operating
philosophy

Four pillars built into every program, every camp, every scholarship. Not a mission statement — a way of moving through the world.

  • 01

    Educational
    Wellness

    Education is the foundation of opportunity. We support youth beyond the classroom by building confidence, discipline, and a belief in their own potential.

  • 02

    Physical
    Wellness

    Movement changes lives. Through basketball and active programming, we promote healthy habits, teamwork, and resilience — not just physically, but in character.

  • 03

    Mental
    Wellness

    Strong minds build strong futures. We create spaces where youth feel seen, supported, and valued — helping young people navigate challenges and know they are never alone.

  • 04

    Community
    Service

    It takes a village to raise leaders. We invest directly into communities by creating safe, inclusive spaces and empowering the next generation to give back.
