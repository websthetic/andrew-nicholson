2012 NBA Draft · Orlando Magic

Drafted.
Not given.

Andrew Nicholson was selected 19th overall by the Orlando Magic in the 2012 NBA Draft, the culmination of a career built on discipline and relentless preparation. His jersey number, 44, was retired by St. Bonaventure University and he is an inductee of their Athletics Hall of Fame.

#19 2012 NBA Draft Pick
Seoul Samsung Thunders · KBL

Longevity defined
by excellence.

Since 2017, Andrew has competed continuously across elite leagues in Asia. He currently plays for the Seoul Samsung Thunders in the Korean Basketball League, a career defined not just by where it started, but by the standard it has maintained across every organisation he has represented.
