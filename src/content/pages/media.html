What Andrew Speaks About

Speaking
Topics.

Andrew draws from 13 years as a professional athlete and active founder to deliver talks that are practical, personal, and grounded in real experience.

Business

Athlete Entrepreneurship

Building a business portfolio while still competing professionally. What discipline, preparation, and a long-term mindset actually look like in practice — not in theory.

Wellness & Performance

The Business of Recovery

Recovery and wellness are not trends — they are the next frontier of performance science. Why every serious athlete and executive needs to think about recovery differently.

Community & Foundation

Community Investment

How to turn a platform into lasting community impact. Lessons from founding 44 Hoops Foundation during his active NBA career — and what it means to build for others, not just yourself.

Nutrition & Lifestyle

Clean Eating & Active Living

Peak performance starts on the plate. Andrew shares how the habits of elite athletes — intentional nutrition, consistent movement, and clean fueling — translate directly into sharper thinking and better decisions.

Transition & Mindset

Life After the Game

The transition every athlete faces — and how to prepare for it before you have to. The mindset shift from competitor to builder, and why starting early changes everything.
