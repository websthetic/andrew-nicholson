Andrew Nicholson

Andrew Nicholson is a former professional basketball player turned entrepreneur and investor with a diverse portfolio spanning sports, wellness, biotech, nutrition, hospitality, and community impact.

Who is Andrew

Physics graduate. Former NBA professional. Founder, investor, and community builder. Andrew Nicholson's story isn't just about basketball, it's about what happens when discipline, science, and purpose converge into something bigger than the game.

More Than an Athlete

Impact

Build

Before the courts, there was the classroom. Andrew's foundation in physics at St. Bonaventure University gave him something most athletes never develop, a systems thinker's mind. The ability to break down complexity, identify patterns, and solve for the unknown. It's the same principle he applies to every venture he touches.

Compete

Eight seasons in the NBA teaches you things no business school can. The relentless preparation, the resilience through adversity, the discipline to show up when it counts. Andrew carried every lesson from the professional game into how he builds with consistency, competitiveness, and an unwillingness to settle for average.

Create

Andrew doesn't write cheques and walk away. He embeds himself from concept to launch, from brand identity to operations. Across sports, wellness, biotech, hospitality, and media, he builds businesses with the same hands-on intensity he brought to every training session. The venture is the arena. And he plays to win.
